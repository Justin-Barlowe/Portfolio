About Me

Welcome! I'm Justin Barlowe, a passionate web development student at Bellevue University, striving to hone my skills as a FullStack Developer. My enthusiasm for technology started early, evolving into a lifelong commitment. Back in 2008, my journey began when I delved into the realms of HTML, CSS, and PHP, creating engaging video game clan websites for Counter-Strike and Americas Army.

Following high school, I served as a Combat Medic in the Army, all the while maintaining my interest in Web Development, Network Engineering, and Cybersecurity. Upon my departure from the Army in 2013, I found fulfillment in the medical field. I proceeded to Paramedic school and have since held a variety of roles over the past decade, including Paramedic, Paramedic Supervisor, Tactical Medic, and Medical Examiner.

Currently, I'm based in Cameron, North Carolina, sharing my home with my beloved wife Andrea, our three children, and an assortment of dogs, cats, and snakes. Despite the years and my varied professional journey, my passion for the digital world never waned. I'm not just a web development enthusiast but also an avid gamer. Besides that, you can often find me at metal festivals, or experimenting with recipes in the kitchen. My diverse interests and experiences shape my unique perspective in web development, fueling my desire to create and innovate.
